Indian schoolteacher bags $1 mn Global Teacher Prize

 
 

Teachers are ‘the world’s real change-makers’. Carrying this belief in his heart, 32-year-old schoolteacher Ranjitsinh Disale has made a big difference to a small village in Maharashtra. For his efforts, Disale has been awarded the Global Teacher Prize for 2020. 

What did Disale do? Check out the app’s News section for these informational tidbits!

💡 What did Disale do to make learning enjoyable for his students? 

  1. He replaced the traditional indoor classroom with an outdoor one. 
  2. He gave the students access to digital resources. 
  3. He built a computer café in the village. 
  4. He built a library in the village

Building the core language skills

Words are important building blocks of communication. The sum total of words that one learns and knows when and where to use, is called vocabulary. In other words, building one’s vocabulary is an integral part of language learning and communication. 

Head over to the app’s Activities section to help your child build and expand their vocabulary with fun and interactive activities!
